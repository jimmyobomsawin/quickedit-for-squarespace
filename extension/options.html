QuickEdit for Squarespace

For each website you own, map its public address to its Squarespace subdomain. A small pencil will appear in the top-left corner of any page on a mapped website. Click it to open that page in the Squarespace editor.

Tips

  • Import from Squarespace: open account.squarespace.com, let it load, then come back here and click Import. The extension pulls each site's name, subdomain, and primary custom domain from your dashboard.
  • Website: just the domain — e.g. jimmytechsf.com. https://, www., and trailing slashes are stripped automatically.
  • Squarespace subdomain: the part before .squarespace.com in your admin URL. Pasting the full URL is fine too; everything except the subdomain gets stripped.
  • You need to be signed in to Squarespace in this browser for the editor to load when you click the overlay.
